Optical illusions

Once I heard: Nobuyoshi Araki was sleeping with all the women he photographed. It's also a question that people ask you when you take pictures of naked women. We live in a world of appearances, where the mind is carried away by what it can see and not by proper research.

This is a series of photos that I have proposed some friends with the following description: "(...) you appear naked in a bed and semi covered with sheets. I as a photographer will be somewhere in the bed covered from the waist down, with the torso naked and holding my camera taking us a selfie in the mirror. There is no physical contact between us. The idea is to make the viewer think that there was a sexual encounter, when in fact I am wearing pants and nothing has happened. At the end of the series of photographs will be the version where there are no sheets and you can see that everything was an illusion."
